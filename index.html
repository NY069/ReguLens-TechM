Tri-Agent Architecture

Retrieve. Synthesize. Verify. Every claim is traceable to source.

📚
Horizon Ingestion
Agent I — Retrieval
Parses EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001. Chunks text, embeds via nomic-embed-text, stores in PostgreSQL + pgvector. Cosine similarity ≥ 0.45 retrieves top 3–5 spans per query.
pgvector Ollama PostgreSQL Chunking
✨
Persona Synthesis
Agent II — Synthesis
Generates persona-aware answers grounded in retrieved context. Five personas: Lead Auditor, ML Engineer, Legal Counsel, HR Auditor, QA Engineer. Backends: Ollama or Groq Cloud.

Agent III — Verification

Evaluation Framework

Six dimensions of compliance intelligence, rigorously measured.

01
Retrieval Accuracy
Top-3 span recall across all three regulatory frameworks. Cosine similarity threshold tuned per domain.
02
Synthesis Fidelity
Answer grounding in retrieved context. Persona-specific tone calibration. Zero hallucination rate.
03
NLI Precision
Atomic claim verification via Natural Language Inference. Threshold tuning per persona (0.88–0.96).
04
Audit Trail Integrity
Immutable ACID logging. Every query, verdict, and source reference timestamped and versioned.
05
Staleness Detection
Agent IV monitors regulatory feeds. Flags outdated corpus entries. Emergency ingestion on critical updates.
06
Model Fairness
Fairness metrics validation vs EU AI Act Art. 6–27, NIST pillars, ISO 42001 §6–9. HR + Manufacturing domains.
